About the Team

The BookWorm digital library is an online database platform designed for residents of New Jersey to have access to dozens of digital books. The platform provides a responsible and intuitive user interface to make renting a digital book extremely simple. Anyone browsing the library at there leisure may view the dozens of books on offer. If a user chooses to make an account, after a quick sign up, members may rent digital books as well as create reading lists for future titles. The structure of the library itself was designed by a team of ambition rutgers students as a final class project.
